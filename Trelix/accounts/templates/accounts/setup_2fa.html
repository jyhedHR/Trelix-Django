What is Two-Step Verification?

Two-step verification adds an extra layer of security to your account. After entering your password, you'll need to enter a 6-digit code from your authenticator app to complete the login process.

How it works:
  1. Download an authenticator app like Google Authenticator, Authy, or Microsoft Authenticator
  2. Scan the QR code that will be shown on the next page
  3. Save your backup codes in a safe place
  4. Verify the setup by entering a code from your authenticator app
Important: Make sure you have an authenticator app installed on your mobile device before proceeding.
